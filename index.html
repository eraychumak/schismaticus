A Very Unique Game.

¤ DESCRIPTION ¤

--------------------

A one player game, you start as one part but you can get split into two if you hit an obstacle.

Splitting increases difficulty and makes it harder for you to gain a higher score.

Obstacle speed will decrease when you go right vice-versa.

¤ HOW TO PLAY ¤

--------------------

Play a few rounds and get the hang of it. Eventually, you'll become a pro!


CONTROLS

  • LEFT ARROW - move left.
  • RIGHT ARROW - move right.
  • SPACE - jump.

¤ FUTURE PLANS ¤

--------------------

Fix a few bugs here and there - make things more smoother and playable.

You can input your name which then will be used to save your high-score.

There will be a global leaderboard.
